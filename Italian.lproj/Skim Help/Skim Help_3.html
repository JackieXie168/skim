Magnifying a section of a PDF document

You can live-magnify a section of the PDF file, for example to better view small text.

To magnify a section:

  1. Choose Tools > Magnify Tool.
  2. Press the left mouse button while moving over the section you want to magnify.

To modify the magnification:

  • Hold the Apple key for extra magnification.
  • Hold the Option key and/or double-click to magnify a larger area.
  • Hold the Shift key to zoom out (invert the magnification).
