Check for updates of Skim

Skim can automatically check whether an update for Skim is available. You can also choose how often Skim checks for updates.

  • To select how often Skim checks for updates, choose Skim > Preferences, click General, and select a period after "Check for Skim updates".

You can also check immediately for Skim updates yourself. Choose Skim > Check for Updates....
