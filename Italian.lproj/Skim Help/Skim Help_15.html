Viewing pages side-by-side

Viewing pages side-by-side lets you display more of a document on the screen.

  • To view two pages side-by-side, choose PDF > PDF Display > Facing Pages. If you want the first page to be a right-hand page, as it would be in a book, choose PDF > PDF Display > Book Mode. Otherwise, the first page will be a left-hand page.
  • To stop viewing pages side-by-side, choose PDF > PDF Display > Single Pages.
