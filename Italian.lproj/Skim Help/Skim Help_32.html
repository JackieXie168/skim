Showing the reading bar

In skim you can highlight the line of text you are reading, to help you concentrate on reading or easily find back what you were reading.

  • To show the reading bar, choose View > Show Reading Bar.
  • To move the reading bar to the next (previous) line, hold down the Option key and press the Down Arrow (Up Arrow) key.
  • To move the reading bar to the next (previous) page, hold down the Option key and press the Right Arrow (Left Arrow) key.
  • To increase (decrease) the number of lines the reading bar spans, hold down the Option and Shift keys and press the Down Arrow (Up Arrow) key.

You can also drag the reading bar to another line.
